United Porte

Online furniture store in the USA

United Porte is a manufacturer and supplier of doors, windows, and furniture in the USA. The main task was to update the website design with a focus on enhancing product customization. Now, users can not only change the size and color of selected items but also adjust additional parameters such as glass type, locks, hinges, and more.

In addition to introducing new functionality, the redesign involved optimizing all pages of the online store to improve the user experience and increase conversion rates.

On the category page, promotional banners are occasionally integrated into the product list to subtly inform customers about discounts and special offers. This approach helps draw attention to promotions without being overly intrusive.

Previously, customers had to choose doors from a general category, but if a specific detail, such as the lock mechanism or hinges, didn’t fit, they had to search for another model in the overall list. This significantly complicated the user flow and made the selection process less convenient.

Therefore, a new feature has been added to the product page, allowing customers to customize doors by changing the hinges, glass type, lock type and design, as well as the color and material of the door. Now, buyers can easily adjust the doors to match their interior design and personal.

A unique icon has been developed for each product category and subcategory to help future customers quickly understand the functionality of the doors. With visual cues, selecting the right mechanism becomes intuitive, significantly simplifying the process of searching and comparing models.

The shopping cart also highlights the option to purchase additional services, such as door assembly or installation. This not only simplifies the purchasing process for the customer but also increases the revenue from offering these services.
