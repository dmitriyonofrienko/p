Shemax

Manufacturer of equipment and accessories for the beauty industry.

Shemax is a Ukrainian company that manufactures equipment and accessories for the beauty industry. A new website for the manufacturer of beauty equipment, presenting branded products with an emphasis on them.

The site has two main categories of users:

  • Owners and managers of beauty salons who order goods with delivery for use in their daily work,
  • Foreign distributors who order bulk quantities of goods for sale in their regions.

Therefore, we had to make the site as beautiful as possible so that the owners would want to buy, and convenient so that distributors could make large purchases.
