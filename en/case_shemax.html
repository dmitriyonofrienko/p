Shemax

Manufacturer of Beauty Equipment and Accessories

Shimax is a Ukrainian company specializing in the production of beauty equipment and accessories. The new website showcases the brand’s products with a strong emphasis on their quality and design.

The site serves two main user categories:

  • Salon owners and managers – who order products for daily use with direct delivery.
  • International distributors – who purchase wholesale batches for resale in their respective regions.

The goal was to create a visually appealing website that encourages salon owners to buy while ensuring a seamless experience for distributors making bulk purchases.

Each page is filled with pre-prepared components: description, product gallery, video presentation, product characteristics, equipment, and important product features.

This way, each page can be made unique and different from the others, emphasizing the features specific to each position.
