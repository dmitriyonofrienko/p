For Woman In Science

Website of a non-profit organization

The L'Oréal-UNESCO International Prize “For Women in Science” annually recognizes three outstanding women scientists in Ukraine. This year's winners will receive UAH 300,000 for their significant contribution to scientific progress in the life sciences or in the fields of physical sciences, mathematics and computer science.

To hold the prize, a website must be created that will include an application form, as well as information about past events, participants, and jury members.
